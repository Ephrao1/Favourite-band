Sauti Sol

Sauti Sol is a Kenyan Afro-pop band.It consists of: Baraza-Vocalist, Willis
Chimano and Savara Mudigi who were initially in an a capella group and lastly and
Polycarp Otieno-Guitarist. The band was formed in 2005.
It started off as an a capella group by Baraza,
Chimano and Savara who had met in highschool(Upper Hill High School).
They were in the same music group at the school known as Voices in the Light.
met guitarist Otieno at the Alliance Francaise and have been together since then.
